Mandarin Marketer

Mandarin Marketer is an idea that I came up with alongside one of my close Chinese friends from Yunnan Province in the People's Republic of China.

It would be a marketing firm that would specifically help Chinese and American small and medium-sized businesses market their products and services to one another. The firm would navigate the Great Firewall of China, and help underdog businesses across the Pacific reach foreign-language clientele.

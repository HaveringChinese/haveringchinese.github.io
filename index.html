Mandarin Marketer

Mandarin Marketer is an idea that I came up with alongside one of my close Chinese friends from Yunnan Province in the People's Republic of China.

It would be a marketing firm that would specifically help Chinese and American small and medium-sized businesses market their products and services to one another.

Typically, only much larger corporations succeed at this.

The firm would navigate the Great Firewall of China, and help underdog businesses across the Pacific reach foreign-language clientele.

The site uses custom CSS and JavaScript, and incorporates data pulled in real time from several APIs.

Hempn

Hempn is an e-commerce platform designed to help customers easily start buying more carbon-neutral and carbon-negative hemp-based products.

It features a user-friendly tool for calculating how much revenue an individual could generate by growing hemp in currently unused urban spaces or on unproductive land.

In addition, the shopping cart updates to reflect the amount of CO2 sequestered by the products to be purchased. Shopping just got even more fun!

The site itself is built using React and Express.js.
